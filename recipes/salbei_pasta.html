Pasta with sage

Description

Delicious pasta with sage. Done in under 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 ml olive oil
  • 10 fresh sage leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable broth
  • 150g of your favourite pasta
  • water from cooked pasta
  • garlic
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water
  2. Cut garlic, warm up with olive oil in a pan
  3. Add a cup of the water from the pasta pot to the pan and add vegetable broth
  4. Add sage leaves and pepper
  5. Put the pasta into the pan and let it sit for 2 minutes
  6. Serve with parmesan
